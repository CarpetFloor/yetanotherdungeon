-Dungeon with different randomly generated rooms
-Enemies drop coins
-Start at center location that has a shop
-The further out the player gets, the harder the enemies become, but the bigger the reward
-Only way to get health and better equipment at shops
-Shops start to generate further out
-Going into new location reveals it on the map
-Shops are a safe zone, meaning that enemies can't spawn, but when entering, already cleared rooms 
	regenerate enemies
-Medieval style combat and weapons
